To-Do App

Instructions: First, create a new list by typing its name into the "New List" input box and clicking "Create List" or hitting the Enter key. Click on the newly created list's name to be taken to the new list. Once inside the new list, type your to-do item in the "New To-Do" input box and click "Add" or hit the Enter key to submit it. To edit a to-do item, double-click on the item's name, edit the text as desired, and hit the Enter key to submit. To mark a to-do item as completed, click the checkbox to the right of the item. To remove a list or a to-do item, click the minus symbol to the left of the list or to-do item's name. To remove all completed to-do items, click the "Clear Completed To-Dos" button.
